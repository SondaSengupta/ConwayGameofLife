Conway's Game of Life

This game is a zero-state game in which no further input is required after initially creating the board. There are three rules:

  1. A cell lives if it has 2-3 neighboring cells around it
  2. A dead cell regenerates if there are 3 neighboring cells around it.
  3. Cells with lower than 2 or higher than 3 living neighbors dies.
Based off John Conway's game published in 1970. Living cells are blue and dead cells are grey.
